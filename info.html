Primary Nutrients

Primary nutrients include nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, which are essential for plant growth and development.

Secondary Nutrients

Secondary nutrients like magnesium, calcium, and sulfur play a supportive role in improving soil and plant health.

Herbicides

Herbicides control unwanted weeds that compete with crops for nutrients and water.

Insecticides

Insecticides help manage harmful insects, protecting crops from damage and improving yields.

Fungicides

Fungicides prevent and treat fungal infections, ensuring healthier plants and higher productivity.

Plant Growth Regulators

Plant growth regulators enhance the efficiency of nutrient use and promote root and shoot development.

Microbial Inoculants

Microbial inoculants improve nutrient uptake, soil health, and resistance to stress conditions.
