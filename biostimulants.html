Organic Biostimulants

Organic biostimulants are derived from natural sources, such as plant extracts, seaweed, and humic substances. They enhance nutrient absorption, improve plant growth, and increase resistance to stress.

Inorganic Biostimulants

Inorganic biostimulants are synthesized products that include mineral-based compounds. They enhance plant metabolism, support growth, and improve overall crop yield efficiency.
